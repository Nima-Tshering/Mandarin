About Mandarin Hills Homestay

Located just 25 minutes away from the cosmopolitan town of Gangtok.The Mandarin Hills Homestay is here to provide you with not just a "home away from home" experience, but a whole lot more.Excluding warmth of a family run homestay ."excellent customer service and hygiene" is our utmost priority and a guiding force behind the uniqueness of the Mandarin Hills Homestay. We strive to offers our visitors a unique experience of the Sikkimese culture, tradition and hospitality at the best

More Information

Watching the mist settle down by the window as you sip your morning tea with homw cooked breakfast. Hearing the sweet twitter of birds of rare species as you wake up is what Mandarin Hills Homestay is what the place has to offer you.A lovely ambience with welcoming smile to greet you, you may find another home in these mountains of Sikkim Himalayas. Nestled between the mountains this place allows you a perfect balance of rural and urban set up. A few light hike down the valley and you will be greeted by a lovely brook where you can camp and go for bird watching. Surrounded by rare species of trees and bamboo groves, this place allows you to feel the comfort and taste of wilderness that the mountains would want to give you.
